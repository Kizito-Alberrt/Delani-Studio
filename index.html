About us

Over 7 years, we have worked on a variety of projects offering insight driven design solutions which are thoughtful, innovative and well researched. Our designs are customized to our clients needs. We follow a process driven and immersive approach in our work and take time to get to know our clients and their brands. We do not promise overnight design solutions, but can guarantee a fresh perspective and an enthusiastic team. We approach each project with curiosity, a lot of questions and multi-directional perspective to solving design problems. We strongly believe that good design emerges from collaboration and a variety of different perspectives, which come together to create a strong, insight driven and long lasting brand. For us, each project is a partnership between the client and the designer.




Services

provides analog and digital Recording and Mixing services. We work with different genres, rock, pop, punk, dance, electro, folk, jazz, r&b, choral, orchestral.

When recording at our facilities it's possible to obtain a vast variety of sounds thanks to two recording rooms with different sound characteristics and a wide selection of instruments at the musicians' disposal. Our objective is to work with you towards the obtainment of your ideal sound.
